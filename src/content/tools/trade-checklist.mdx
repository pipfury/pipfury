## Before entry
Write the context, evidence, thesis, invalidation, risk, and trigger.

## Required checks
Is the setup inside the plan? Is invalidation clear? Is risk acceptable? Is the trigger present? Is the trade being taken to recover a loss?

## Pass or fail
If any core field is missing, the trade fails the checklist.

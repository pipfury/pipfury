## Journal fields
Instrument, session, timeframe, setup, risk, invalidation, entry reason, outcome, emotion, mistake, lesson, and rule adjustment.

## Reflection prompts
What did I see? What did I assume? What invalidated the idea? What did I repeat well? What should I stop?

## Lesson
A journal is not punishment. It is how the trader turns experience into evidence.

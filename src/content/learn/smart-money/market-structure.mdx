## Structure is context
Market structure helps the trader identify whether price is trending, ranging, transitioning, or rejecting a prior phase.

## Breaks need quality
A break of structure is more useful when the break has displacement, location, and follow-through. A small poke beyond a level may be only liquidity.

## Multi-timeframe caution
Lower timeframe structure can conflict with higher timeframe context. The higher timeframe often defines the broader risk environment.

## Practice
Map highs and lows on two timeframes before reading any setup.

## The rule before the method
PipFury begins with a boundary: analysis is not a signal. A market read is useful only when the trader can identify context, evidence, invalidation, risk, and review.

## The study sequence
Start with candles, volume, spread, close, support, resistance, and basic risk. Then move into VSA, liquidity, structure, execution, and psychology.

## What to record
For every chart, write the read, the evidence, the invalidation, the outcome, and the lesson. This turns market observation into skill instead of dependency.

## Practice checklist
Before studying any setup, ask: What is confirmed? What is assumption? Where is the idea wrong? What is the risk? What will I review later?

## The read
Price swept prior liquidity and then failed to produce clean continuation. The educational question was whether effort was producing result or whether the market was rejecting after the sweep.

![XAUUSD chart](/images/charts/xauusd-liquidity-raid-case-file.svg)

## The evidence
The key evidence was location at prior liquidity, a visible push, weak follow-through, and the need for confirmation before any trade decision.

## The invalidation
The read is wrong if price accepts above the marked zone and continues with healthy spread and close. Invalidation must be written before bias hardens.

## The lesson
A liquidity raid is context. It becomes useful only when the trader can separate evidence, assumption, and invalidation.

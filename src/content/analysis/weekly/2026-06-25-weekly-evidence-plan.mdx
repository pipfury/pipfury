## The read
This weekly plan identifies areas to observe, not entries to copy. The goal is to prepare questions for the chart.

## DXY context
DXY should be studied first because major forex pairs often react to broad dollar strength or weakness. The question is whether dollar movement confirms or contradicts pair-level structure.

## Gold context
For XAUUSD, mark prior liquidity, obvious highs and lows, and zones where effort may fail to produce result.

## The lesson
A weekly outlook should reduce impulsive trading. It should not create pressure to predict.

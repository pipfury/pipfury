## Pre-trade thesis
The market had swept liquidity and appeared to slow. The context was reasonable, but context was not a trigger.

## What went right
Risk was defined before entry. The stop was accepted. The loss was contained.

## What went wrong
The entry happened before confirmation. The trader moved from thesis to trigger too quickly.

## Rule adjustment
After a liquidity raid, wait for confirmation. Good context does not excuse poor timing.
